## 摘要
我们研究了在右端边界条件下，具有共变导数的一阶路径依赖 Hamilton-Jacobi 方程柯西问题的极小极大（广义）解。在比以往文献中考虑的更为一般的哈密顿量假设下（特别是允许对第一个（时间）变量具有可测依赖性），我们建立了极小极大解的存在性、唯一性、稳定性和相容性结果。作为应用，我们考虑了一个时滞系统的零和微分对策，并证明了在比已知假设更一般、但与 Carathéodory 条件一致且相当自然的假设下，该博弈具有值。

## Abstract
We study minimax (generalized) solutions of a Cauchy problem for a (first-order) path-dependent Hamilton--Jacobi equation with co-invariant derivatives under a right-end boundary condition. Under assumptions on the Hamiltonian that are more general than those previously considered in the literature and allow, in particular, a measurable dependence on the first (time) variable, we establish existence, uniqueness, stability, and consistency results for minimax solutions. As an application, we consider a zero-sum differential game for a time-delay system and prove that this game has a value under assumptions more general than the known ones but rather natural being consistent with the Carathéodory conditions.
